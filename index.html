Understanding Your Options for Recovery

You're taking an important first step by researching treatment options. Recovery starts with information, and we're here to help you understand what's available.

Treatment Isn't One-Size-Fits-All

At Pinnacle Recovery, we offer a range of programs designed to meet you where you are:

  • Residential Treatment (30-90 days) — Live at our facility with 24/7 care and support
  • Intensive Outpatient Program (3 evenings/week) — Continue daily life while getting structured support
  • Medically-Supervised Detox — Safe withdrawal management under 24/7 medical supervision
  • Dual Diagnosis Care — Treat addiction and co-occurring mental health together
  • Aftercare & Alumni Support — Recovery doesn't end at discharge

What Makes Treatment Successful

Research shows the most effective programs combine:

  • Evidence-based therapies (CBT, EMDR, trauma-informed care)
  • Holistic healing (yoga, meditation, nutritional support, nature therapy)
  • Personalized treatment plans — not cookie-cutter approaches
  • Small client-to-staff ratios for individual attention
  • Aftercare support — because recovery is a lifelong journey
